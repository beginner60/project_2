Athens is a city and the county seat of Athens County, Ohio, United States.

Athens is the home of Ohio University, a large public research university with an undergraduate and graduate enrollment of more than 21,000 students.

Located along the Hocking River in the southeastern part of Ohio about 65 miles (105 km) southeast of Columbus
